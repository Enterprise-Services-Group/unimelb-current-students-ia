## Key services provided by the hub

| Service category | Hub provides |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Course admin** | Enrolment, re-enrolment, course planning, timetable, key dates, exams, results, graduation, leave, withdrawal, cross-institutional study, study overseas |
| **Student support** | Stop 1, Stop 1 AI, health, counselling, disability services, financial aid, scholarships, international support |
| **Student life** | Campus life, peer mentoring, Melbourne Plus, volunteering, food, transport, study spaces, clubs (via UMSU/GSA) |
| **Careers** | Employability framework, Career Checklist, Careers Online, Smart Resume, cover letters, interviews, career advice |
| **Academic skills** | PASS, academic skills resources |

## What the hub does NOT cover (faculty-specific content)

Based on cross-faculty comparison, the hub does NOT cover:
- **Faculty-specific course plans** (MSD, Arts, Science, Law all have their own)
- **Placement/practicum management** (Education placements, MDHS clinical, Law clinics, MSD internships)
- **Discipline-specific academic skills** (Law legal academic skills, Education academic skills module)
- **Faculty-specific forms** (MSD list of forms, FFAM ensemble forms)
- **Studio/performance/practice management** (MSD studios, FFAM practice rooms, ensembles)
- **Program-specific regulatory requirements** (LANTITE for Education, Fitness to Practice for MDHS)
- **Faculty scholarships and prizes** (MSD, Science, MDHS all have separate prize pages)
- **Faculty-specific student enrichment** (MSD mentoring, Science ambassadors, MBS career elevation)
- **School-level course planning** (Biomedical Sciences BBiomed planning)
