What this means

1. Low student-journey priority

Sport is not a core academic service. It doesn't appear in any of the 5 persona journeys. The 67 links from 12 crawled domains are chrome-level, not journey-critical. This crawl is low analytical value for the current-students IA project.

2. The best page-weight performance in the estate

67KB median is exceptional. The other domains range from 143KB (services) to 304KB (study). MU Sport is the exemplar for lightweight, fast-loading pages.

3. Strong transactional connection

The 29 ecommerce links show a working payment integration — rare in the estate where most actions are behind walls. Students can actually transact on the sport site.

4. The sport ecosystem is independent but connected

MU Sport links to the corporate site, ecommerce, and giving — it's integrated with the University's operational systems even though it's a separate service. This is the model that counselling and Safer Community should follow.
